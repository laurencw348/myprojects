General Information

Although I have an Australian passport, I was born and (mostly) raised in the city of Hong Kong- the place I call home. I did live in Australia briefly for about 5 years before moving back to Hong Kong when I was 11 years old. For high school, I decided to upend my life and go to boarding school in Singapore. Although it was daunting to leave my parents and be on my own for the first time, it was the best decision I ever made. I met amazing people and had incredible experiences all in the beautiful city of Singapore.

In my free time, I love to bake, run/hike, listen to music, play the clarinet and play with my dog Tyler. I also love to both watch and make films. In my free time, I making and editing short films with my friends. I also love acting: during my high school, I participated in several productions that were both rewarding and exciting.

Work Experiences and Skills

Over the last 6 years, I have had the opportunity to gain work experience in a number of exciting and fast paced industries. Most notably, I intered at CNN in the summer of 2018 during the Hong Kong protests. While working at CNN, I was able to gain valuable exposure to field of journalism while learning from experts. In the summer of 2020, I also worked at a startup called ZMS Education: there, I was able to learn the ins and outs of how a tech start up works. Through my experiences, I was able to also hone valuable technical skills, such as programming, accounting and planning. Further, I also gained soft skills that such as teamwork, time management , creativity, and self motivation.
