IB and IGSE Subject Online Tutoring

Welcome! I'm Lauren and I tutor high school students on a wide range of IB and IGCSE subjects. I help students build confidence and excel in their internal assessments, essays and final exams.

Through Zoom or Skype, we will work together to using an online whiteboard so all you need to get started is a computer with working microphone and camera.
